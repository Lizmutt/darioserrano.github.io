Androgynous Spacecraft Docking Mechanism

Kinematics & Dynamics of Machines • Sep–Dec 2025

What I built

  • Parametric CAD of a 6-arm variable-angle rough-alignment docking interface
  • MATLAB kinematic + static analysis to compute cone half-angle vs slider travel (0.40–0.45 m)
  • 6×6 equilibrium solve for joint reactions under a 6 kN axial docking load (1000 N per arm)
  • Jacobian-based force mapping for actuator loads (Stewart-platform style)

Key results

  • Docking axial load case: 6 kN
  • Required slider actuator force: ≈ 2.17 kN
